# 11. Future Enhancements & New Modules

Currently, SPORTS serves as an isolated, personalized intelligence layer. To multiply student engagement, moving from a "Single-Player" to a "Multiplayer" ecosystem is the logical next step.

## Proposed New Tabs (Current Interface Extensions)

### 1. The "Code Colosseum" (New Tab)
- **Concept:** An embedded, browser-based IDE (using Monaco Editor/repl.it API).
- **Functionality:** Instead of just generating a test with multiple-choice questions in the "Test Lab", students are given a live React/Python container. If an Opportunity matches them to Google, the system pulls a Google LeetCode-style question, and they must write passing unit tests directly in the browser. 

### 2. "Alumni Connect network" (New Tab)
- **Concept:** A mentorship matchmaking system.
- **Functionality:** Since SPORTS possesses the verified skills of thousands of seniors and juniors. The system can automatically map a Freshman who wants to learn "React" with a Senior who attained a 90% score in the "React Test Lab". They can sync via an in-app chat widget.

### 3. Advanced Gamification & Leaderboards (Dashboard Enhancement)
- **Concept:** "Regional / College-Wide Rankings."
- **Functionality:** Add a leaderboard widget to the OverviewTab. Based on Test Lab scores, Hackathon placements, and fast-track learning completions, generate a global ELO rating. Visualizing where a student ranks within their graduating class fosters hyper-engagement.

### 4. Real-time Interview Sandbox (Prep Extension)
- **Concept:** AI-Driven Voice Interviews.
- **Functionality:** In the Prep tab, integrate standard browser WebRTC microphones. Use the OpenAI/Gemini voice models to physically simulate a technical recruiter. It asks questions, the student replies vocally, and the AI evaluates hesitation, structured thinking, and technical accuracy, providing an instant scorecard.
